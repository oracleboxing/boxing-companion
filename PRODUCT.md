# Product Brief

## Name

**Boxing Companion**

Brand relationship: **Boxing Companion by Oracle Boxing**.

Use `Boxing Companion` as the app/product name. Use Oracle Boxing as the parent brand and trust signal.

## Core promise

Open the app, know what to train, press Start Workout, follow the session.

## Target user

Primary user:
- Oracle Boxing Skool member
- beginner to intermediate boxer
- wants clear structure and coaching direction
- may train at home, in a gym, or around bag/shadowboxing sessions

They are not looking for a generic workout app. They want Oracle Boxing's coaching system in their pocket.

## Product principles

1. **Start Workout is the main action**
   - The app should always make the next training step obvious.

2. **Coach-led, not AI mush**
   - AI may help draft, cluster, and organise internal content.
   - Final canonical training content should feel authored by Oracle Boxing.

3. **Premium and calm**
   - Apple-style clarity.
   - Minimal surfaces.
   - No noisy gamification.

4. **Small elite library over huge weak catalogue**
   - Better to have 30 excellent movements/workouts than 300 generic ones.

5. **Skool companion, not Skool replacement**
   - Community, announcements, and discussion stay in Skool.
   - The app handles training execution and progress.

## MVP narrative

A member opens Boxing Companion and sees what to train today. They press Start Workout. The app guides them through a warm-up, boxing rounds, rest periods, and clear cues. At the end, it records completion and helps them keep momentum.
